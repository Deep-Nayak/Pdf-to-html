FLATBED TRAILER

SPECIFICATIONS

Trailer weight (Unladen)

7.7 T (APPROX)

 

 

Chassis

All steel welded construction. 2 Nos Main longitudinal members

 

are high strength lighter weight Parallel flange Beam running

 

along full length of the trailer. Cross members - Rolled or Folded

 

of material IS-2062/ST-52 are positioned at suitable locations.

 

3.15 MS chequered sheet platform. 12 nos of twist lock shall be

 

provided to suit 1- 40 feet container, 2 – 20 feet container and 1-

 

20 feet container at middle.

Landing leg

Axles

king pin

Suspension

Tyre Wheel rim

Brakes

Electrical

Two speed landing leg shall be provided at the front end.

3 Nos. 13T Axle Shall be provided.

2” king pin equivalent to IS : 6763 Part I provided

Heavy Duty Tridem axle multi leaf spring suspension with load equalizing brackets to be provided.

10.00 x 20 – 16 PR – 12 Nos. 7.50 x 20 – 12 Nos.

Twin line air brake system with coupled to tractor brake system. Trailer brakes are actuated from tractor. T 24 brake chamber shall be provided on all axles. Parking brake shall be manually actuated.

24 V DC electrical include tail lamp, brake lamp and direction indicator lamps fitted at rear end of trailer. Side Marker lamps shall be provided on both sides.

Surface preparation

Trailer surface shall be Shot blasted and cleaned.

 

 

Painting

Two coats of Epoxy paint shall be provided with Primer

 

 

Other fitments

Fitments such SUPD, RUPD, reflectors on both sides and rear

 

shall be provided as per CMVR.
